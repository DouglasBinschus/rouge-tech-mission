Mission Statement

Rogue Tech Mission helps service members navigate the transition to civilian careers by providing grounded, AI-supported guidance, visibility into credible transition pathways, and a focused entry point into the technology sector.

Rogue Tech Mission emphasizes clarity over noise by combining practical tools, real-world insight, and disciplined learning to help individuals understand their options, build relevant skills, and take ownership of their transition.

— Douglas Binschus

How This Began

Origins Story

Rogue Tech Mission began as a personal endeavor. After decades in Special Forces, I saw how rapidly technology was reshaping every domain, and how technical competence had become essential.

Preparing for transition, I sought a field that evolves continuously, rewards disciplined learning, and demands problem-solving and effective collaboration with others committed to growth. Technology was the natural next step.

This platform exists to return the support I received by helping others cut through the noise, understand their options, and take ownership of their transition and technical development.
